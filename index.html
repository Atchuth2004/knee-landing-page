Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about knee replacement at Yashoda Hospitals.

What is Knee Replacement Surgery?
Knee replacement surgery removes damaged cartilage and bone from the knee joint and replaces it with an artificial implant to relieve pain and restore movement.
When do I need Knee Replacement Surgery?
It's usually recommended when chronic knee pain and stiffness from arthritis or injury limit daily activities and don't improve with medication or physiotherapy.
What types of knee replacement procedures are available?
Options include robotic-assisted, minimally invasive (MIKRS), total, partial, bilateral, and revision knee replacement, tailored to the patient's condition.
What are the types of knee replacement?
The main types are total knee replacement (TKR) and partial knee replacement (PKR), depending on how much of the joint is affected.
What is the cost of Total Knee Replacement?
Cost varies by implant type, surgical technique and hospital stay. Our team provides a transparent estimate during your consultation.
